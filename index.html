Coca-Cola Bottle Evolution

From vintage glass to today's modern contour.

Old - Early 1900s:

Vintage Glass Bottle

Heavy glass, straight-side beginnings evolving toward the iconic shape. Embossed logos and crown caps defined the earliest Coca-Cola packaging era.

Classic - Mid 1900s:

Classic Contour Glass

The Famous hourglass silhouette becomes standard. Durable glass and a tactile feel made it a world wide icon.

Modern - Today

Modern PET Contour

Lightweight PET keeps the iconic contour while improving portability. Updated labels and closures delivers a fresh, contemporary look.
